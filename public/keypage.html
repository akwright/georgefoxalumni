Newberg is a place many fondly call home. Whether you're a George Fox student, a longtime resident or just passing through, there are countless things to do and see.

Belonging to the George Fox University Alumni Association is free and fosters loyalty to your university, gives you a voice in alumni affairs, provides leadership opportunities, promotes continued excellence in education, and allows us to serve you with benefits.

Benefits for Children of Alumni

As a George Fox alum, your children have lots of opportunities to get involved at George Fox. Everything from sports camps to pen pals, campus visits to outreach experiences, your kids have many ways to experience Bruin life, and it starts right when they are born!

  • Bruin Sports Day
  • Friday@Fox & Campus Visits
