Children of Alumni

What's Available

Children of alumni have several opportunities to get involved at George Fox, and it starts right when they are born! Read about the many ways your child can connect with George Fox University:

Lil' Bruins T-Shirt

Receive a Lil' Bruin T-Shirt when the birth of your baby or the adoption of your child is announced in Journal. Email Alumni Relations, (alumni@georgefox.edu) to notify of the addition to your family within the first year of their birth/adoption.

Penn Pal Kid's Club

Pennington is excited to invite all George Fox University Bruin fans age 12 and younger to join the Penn Pals Kids Club. It's a great way to meet our Bruin coaches and athletes, to see Bruin athletic events for free, to spend time on a university campus at a young age, and to have fun with friends! Learn more

Homecoming

Bring your kids back to your alma mater and they will experience what it’s like to be a Bruin! They will love watching the Bruin athletic games and the many more family-friendly activities at Homecoming.

Athletic Camps

Children of alumni are invited to attend our yearly Bruin Academy athletic camps, which include basketball, volleyball, baseball, softball and soccer camps. Contact Patty Findley at 503-554-2910 or pfindley@georgefox.edu for more information.

Science Outreach Program

These are classes planned primarily for home-school students and are taught by George Fox upperclassmen science students. For more information, you can visit the Science Outreach Program website.

Sports Camps

Spring and summer means sports camp season at George Fox. Youths of all ages are invited to participate in any number of events on campus. Click here to read about what camps are offered this summer.

Friday@Fox & Campus Visits

Thinking about attending George Fox? Come for a Friday@Fox and spend a day in the life of a student. Between campus tours, class visits, department showcases and residence hall tours, you'll get an authentic look at campus life! Call Undergraduate Admissions at 503-554-5540 or go to visit.georgefox.edu for more information.

Bruin Preview

Have the ultimate George Fox visit experience! Stay overnight with a current student, sit in a class, tour the campus, eat in the Bon and attend student events as you get acquainted with George Fox University. Call Undergraduate Admissions at 503-554-5540 or visit bruinpreview.georgefox.edu for more information.

Legacy Program

George Fox University is dedicated to the legacy that children of alumni bring to the school. The goal of this program is to increase the number of legacy students at George Fox because we believe a strong legacy enrollment demonstrates confidence in the institution, its education and its mission. We hope that you and your children will consider George Fox.
